=== HASIL PERBANDINGAN SYNTHETIC IMU vs REAL IMU ===
Tanggal: April 2026

1. Real IMU URFD (sensor fisik, n=39 ADL, 30 Fall):
   ADL  peak: mean=26.17 m/s2
   FALL peak: mean=83.52 m/s2
   Ratio max FALL/ADL = 3.19x

2. OpenPose Synthetic IMU (n=40 ADL, 30 Fall, cam0):
   ADL  mean: 310.2 pixel/s2
   FALL mean: 541.4 pixel/s2
   Ratio mean FALL/ADL = 1.75x
   Ratio max  FALL/ADL = 1.27x

3. MediaPipe Synthetic IMU (n=40 ADL, 30 Fall, cam0):
   ADL  mean: 403.7 pixel/s2
   FALL mean: 1347.2 pixel/s2
   Ratio mean FALL/ADL = 3.34x
   Ratio max  FALL/ADL = 3.18x

KESIMPULAN:
- MediaPipe menghasilkan ratio max 3.18x, hampir identik dengan real IMU (3.19x)
- OpenPose menghasilkan ratio max 1.27x, masih jauh dari real IMU
- MediaPipe JAUH lebih baik dari OpenPose untuk synthetic IMU fall detection
- MediaPipe detection rate 100% vs OpenPose 84.2%
